About AH Nexues Lab

Where Students Move From Learning to Real Internship Experience

AH Nexues Lab Internship Portal is designed as a career bridge for students who want practical exposure before entering the professional market. The portal focuses on guided internship discovery, clean application management and transparent status tracking.

Our Internship Mission

AH Nexues Lab helps students discover roles that match their skills and gives companies a structured way to review fresh talent.

Student Growth Path

Students can create a profile, build a resume preview, take a skill quiz, save opportunities and apply with a cover letter.

Company Workflow

The company panel manages internship posts, applicant queues, interview schedules, feedback and final selection status.

Why This Project Is Strong

The website is divided into student modules and company/admin modules, so both project members can explain separate responsibilities.

Student Member Work

Register, login, dashboard, saved internships, apply page, profile, resume builder and certificates.

Company Member Work

Company dashboard, posting form, application queue, interview scheduling and status updates.

DSA Explanation

Queue handles company applications, stack displays recent activities, and sorting organizes internship results.

Database Explanation

localStorage works as a frontend demo database for users, internships, applications, notifications and quiz scores.
